The Man From Nowhere

And that was the word of GOD.
From the pillaring heights of heaven, to the gaping depths of the sea.
A piercing gaze of the moon,
made the red glow.
The Crimson Dessert, also known as the SEA, bore no fruit
Lifeless, Barren
Dead
Less than dead,
For nothing existed to begin with.
VOID
A metal beheamoth beneath his cracked, leather boots.
Groaning in agony, keeping steadfast
Slicing through
Rest easy child,
Welcome to the ABYSS.
